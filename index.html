About Us

SuperChickens is a decentralized ROI game that is 100% transparent and fair to all players, contract will run forever on Tron blockchain. The mechanics of this dapp ensure that funds in the smartcontract never ends (unlike other games!), this makes SuperChicken the most safe and profitable game ever created. Chicken price is dynamic and autonomously calculated by smartcontract from supply and demand dynamics. Every chicken produce one egg per day. Every day 10% of contract funds (in trx) are distributed in the daily auction. At the same time, every day a raffle drawing is made, and one lucky ticket win the daily raffle jackpot (an amount of chickens). Raffles ticket is always 100 Trx. You can get more chickens whenever you like and earn more ROI from more eggs. For each deposit, contract gives 15% to referrers, 5% to developer, 5% for marketing and the rest is stored in smartcontract for daily auctions distribution. Referral commission is transfered directly to referrer's account.
